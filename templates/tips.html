🌿 Wellness Tips for a Healthy Heart

Follow these simple steps to keep your heart happy 💓

🥗 Eat Healthy

Include more fruits, vegetables, and whole grains. Avoid junk food & too much sugar.

🏃 Stay Active

Exercise at least 30 minutes daily – walking, jogging, yoga, or cycling keeps your heart fit.

💤 Sleep Well

7–8 hours of sleep is essential. It reduces stress and keeps your heart healthy.

🚭 Avoid Smoking

Smoking damages your heart and blood vessels. Quitting smoking improves heart health.

💧 Stay Hydrated

Drink enough water daily. Proper hydration supports blood circulation and heart function.

😊 Manage Stress

Meditation, prayer, or deep breathing can reduce stress and protect your heart.
